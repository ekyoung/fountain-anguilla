Anguilla Island Life

Make memories in Anguilla that will last a lifetime! Water sports, dining and cultural entertainment are just a few of the unforgettable activities available to Fountain guests! Fountain Resort guests can experience:

  • The crystal blue waters of famous Shoal Bay Beach
  • Leisure cruises and boating excursions
  • Snorkeling & diving
  • Deep sea fishing
  • Kayaking
  • Swimming

Cultural activities to experience and visit:

  • The Old Valley for Anguilla's architectural yesteryear
  • Crocus Bay for island military history
  • Heritage Museum for unique island history highlights
  • Island Harbour fishing town
  • Amerindian- Ceremonial Site
  • Wallblake House- Anguilla's only standing plantation house
  • Local Festivals- Moon Splash
