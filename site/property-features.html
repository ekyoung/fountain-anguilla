Fountain Residences Property Features

Fountain Residences Shoal Bay offers guests the premiere east Shoal Bay Beach location in Anguilla! Amazing luxury condominium resort steps to Anguilla's top-ranked beach offers guests unsurpassed Caribbean views, a beach path and easy access to all Shoal Bay's top attractions. Whether you prefer a leisurely beach stroll, swimming in tropical pool paradise, evening dining or entertainment, you can experience it all at the Fountain.

Guest may choose from our Deluxe Studio Suites with much more room than a typical hotel room or our one and two bedroom condominiums with space for friends or family. All residences offer bedrooms with private, en-suite bathrooms, air conditioning and a private balcony or garden terrace.
