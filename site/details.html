Fountain Residence Oceanfront 1 Bedroom

Provides the discriminatory vacationer both privacy and luxury. Over 1,000sqft of oceanfront living space accommodating up to three guests, offers magnificent ocean views from the covered verandah accessible from both the living room and bedroom, private master bedroom retreat with king size bed and premium linens, ensuite double vanity bath with walk-in shower, separate spacious living room and dining area and full upgraded kitchen featuring granite counters and stainless steel appliances.

1 Bedroom Comforts:
• Located in our most oceanfront building
• Flat screen TV in both living room and bedroom
• Fully equipped modern kitchen with upgraded appliances
• Master bedroom suite and bath
• Private covered verandah with ocean view
• Air conditioning
• High speed wireless internet
• Concierge service
• Daily trash and tidy service

Property Features:
• 1,060sqft of living
• Tropical Swimming Pool
• Beach access
• Beach chairs and umbrellas (fee applies)
• Walking distance to dining and entertainment
• Washer and dryer
